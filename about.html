About EcoZ

EcoZ is an educational project designed to raise awareness about sustainability, climate change, and the impact of our daily choices. Through interactive gameplay and accessible information, EcoZ empowers users to make informed decisions for a better future.

Our Mission

  • Promote environmental education and action
  • Encourage sustainable habits in everyday life
  • Foster community and solidarity for a greener world

The Team

  • Developers and educators passionate about the environment
  • Designers committed to accessible, beautiful digital experiences
  • Contributors from diverse backgrounds
